Margherita Pizza

Homemade Margherita Pizza

Nothing but a simple pizza crust, some San Marzano tomatoes, evoo, garlic, fresh mozza and lots of fresh basil. Simple ingredients, yet a seriously flavorful end result!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Canned San Marzano tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Mozzarella
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 550 degrees and let pizza stone rest in oven nearly 1 hour.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan. Add garlic and saute just until lightly golden brown.
  3. Add in tomatoes, season with salt. Simmer about 15 - 20 minutes.
  4. Divide dough into 2 equal portions. Stretch and shape each into an oval.
  5. Lay on floured pizza peel. Spread about 1/2 cup sauce over one crust, sprinkle with half the mozzarella.
  6. Slide onto pizza stone and bake until crust is golden brown and cheese is melted.
  7. Repeat with second crust.
  8. Sprinkle pizzas with fresh basil.
